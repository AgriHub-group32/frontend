1. Overview

Welcome to Agriconnect. This Privacy Policy explains how Agriconnect ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our platform, website, and related services.

By creating an account or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our platform.

🔒 We will never sell your personal data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your information exists solely to help you trade better on Agriconnect.

This policy applies to all users of Agriconnect, including farmers, buyers, logistics partners, and visitors to our website.

📋

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of our platform.

Category Examples How Collected
Identity Data Full name, profile photo Account registration
Contact Data Email address, phone number Account registration
Location Data Region, district, farm address Profile setup, GPS (optional)
Farm / Business Data Crop types, farm size, listings Listings and profile
Transaction Data Prices, quantities, payment records Platform activity
Usage Data Pages visited, search queries, device type Automatically collected
Communication Data Messages between users Platform messaging system

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial account numbers, national ID numbers, or biometric data unless specifically required and consented to.

⚙️

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services — creating and managing your account, processing listings, and facilitating transactions between farmers and buyers.
  • To personalise your experience — showing you relevant market prices, listings, and recommendations based on your region and crop preferences.
  • To communicate with you — sending transaction confirmations, price alerts, support responses, and platform updates.
  • To improve our platform — analysing usage patterns to fix bugs, improve features, and make Agriconnect better for everyone.
  • To ensure safety and security — detecting fraud, verifying user identities, and enforcing our Terms of Use.
  • To comply with legal obligations — maintaining records as required by Ghanaian law and regulations.
⚡ We only use your data for purposes directly related to providing and improving the Agriconnect service. We will always ask for your consent before using your data in any new or unexpected way.
🤝

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited information in the following circumstances:

  • With other platform users — when you create a listing, your farm name, region, and crop information are visible to verified buyers on the platform. Your phone number and email are only shared when you choose to initiate contact.
  • With service providers — we work with trusted third-party providers (such as SMS delivery services, cloud hosting, and payment processors) who assist in operating our platform. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and may only use your data to provide services to us.
  • With logistics partners — if you opt into delivery services, relevant order details are shared with the logistics provider you select.
  • For legal compliance — we may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority in Ghana.
  • In a business transfer — if Agriconnect is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you in advance.

We will never share your personal data with advertisers or allow third-party tracking on our platform for advertising purposes.

🍪

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our platform. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site.

Cookie Type Purpose Can Be Disabled?
Essential Keeping you logged in, maintaining your session No — required for the site to work
Functional Remembering your preferences and region Yes
Analytics Understanding how users navigate the platform Yes
Performance Improving page load speeds and reliability Yes

You can control cookie settings through your browser settings at any time. Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of the platform.

🛡️

6. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement the following measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS) to prevent interception.
  • Passwords are stored using strong one-way hashing — we never store your password in plain text.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised Agriconnect personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • Our servers are hosted on secure, reputable cloud infrastructure with regular security audits.
  • We use OTP (One-Time Password) verification during account creation and sensitive account changes.
⚠️ While we take every precaution, no system is 100% immune to security threats. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@agriconnect.gh.
📦

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your account. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law.
  • Transaction records are retained for up to 7 years to comply with Ghanaian financial and tax regulations.
  • Communication data (messages between users) is retained for 2 years and then permanently deleted.
  • Usage and analytics data is anonymised and retained indefinitely to improve our platform.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@agriconnect.gh.

⚖️

8. Your Rights

As a user of Agriconnect, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — you may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — you may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to deletion — you may request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to object — you may object to how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — you may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent (e.g. marketing emails), you may withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Team at privacy@agriconnect.gh. We will respond within 14 business days.

👨‍👩‍👧

9. Children's Privacy

Agriconnect is intended for use by adults and registered businesses. Our platform is not directed at children under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a child has registered on our platform, please contact us at privacy@agriconnect.gh.

🔄

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Send a notification to your registered email or phone number.
  • Display a prominent notice on our platform for at least 14 days.

Your continued use of Agriconnect after any changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

📬 You can always request a copy of a previous version of this Privacy Policy by emailing us at privacy@agriconnect.gh.
