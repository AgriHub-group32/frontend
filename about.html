Agriconnect was born from a simple belief — that farmers deserve fair access to markets, transparent pricing, and the tools to grow their businesses with confidence.

Our Mission

We exist to close the gap between hardworking farmers and the markets they deserve access to. Too many farmers lose income to middlemen, hidden fees, and a lack of pricing information.

Agriconnect is our answer — a transparent, farmer-first platform that puts the power back where it belongs: in the hands of the people who grow our food.

From smallholder farmers to large-scale producers, we serve everyone with the same commitment to fairness and opportunity.

Our Story

How Agriconnect Began

In 2023, our founders — a group of agricultural engineers and software developers — witnessed firsthand how farmers in rural Ghana were being underpaid for their produce. Buyers would arrive at farm gates with no transparent pricing, and farmers had no way to compare or negotiate.

We set out to change that. What started as a small pilot in the Ashanti Region has grown into a platform connecting thousands of farmers to verified buyers and markets across the country.

Today, Agriconnect is more than a marketplace — it's a movement to ensure that the people who feed the nation are fairly rewarded for their work.
